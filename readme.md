# Remote Work Productivity and Time Management Assistant ⏰

An intelligent productivity assistant designed specifically for remote workers. The application provides personalized suggestions, time management tips, well-being tracking, and AI-powered recommendations to support remote professionals in their daily workflows.

## 📌 Purpose & Goals

The primary purpose of this project is to:
- Enhance the productivity of remote workers.
- Support users in maintaining work-life balance.
- Offer daily planning, recommendations, and well-being monitoring.
- Combine traditional productivity techniques with AI-based suggestions.

---

## 🎯 Features (MVP)

- 📅 **Daily Goals, Tasks & To-Do Lists**
- ⏰ **Smart Time Block Suggestions**
- 🔁 **Pomodoro & Break Optimization**
- 🎵 **AI-curated Music & Focus Playlists**
- 📊 **Mood & Energy Level Tracking**
- 💡 **Daily Motivation & Career Advice**
- 🔄 **Weekly Progress Report via Email**
- 📍 **LinkedIn Integration**
- 🌙 **Chronotype-based Planning (Night Owl or Early Bird)**
- 📘 **Personalized Work Method Suggestions**
- 🧠 **LLM / AI-powered Suggestions and Task Blocks**

---

## 👤 Target Audience

Remote workers and freelancers seeking a better structure for their workday, improved focus, and healthier digital habits.
